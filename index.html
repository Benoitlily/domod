Welcome to DOMOD

Domod Roof undertakes all kinds of roofing installations; new structures with or without timber and re-roofing of existing structures. We install on woodwork; metal purlins as well as Light Gauge Steel Trusses.
We provide our clients with a warranty against leakages for roofs installed by our trained installers.
